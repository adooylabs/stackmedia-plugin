## Production Notes

**Total estimated shot count:** 6 scenes, approximately 12-15 individual shots including B-roll options

**Recommended shooting order (grouped by setup):**
1. Desk setup — Scenes 1, 2, 3, 5, 6 (all at the same desk — shoot in one session, adjust styling between scenes)
2. Phone screen POV — Scene 4 (separate setup, may need phone mount or second person to hold/operate)

**Key props needed:**
- Laptop with multiple browser tabs open (prepared beforehand)
- Phone with FormFocus app installed and demo flow pre-loaded
- Coffee cup (Scene 1 background detail)
- Scattered papers (Scene 1) — clear for Scene 5/6
- Printed to-do list for desk clutter detail

**Creator wardrobe/appearance notes:**
- Scene 1: Casual-but-put-together — a relaxed shirt or blouse, slightly lived-in morning look. Subtle makeup or none. Conveys authenticity.
- Scenes 3-6: Same outfit (continuity) — or a subtle upgrade (neat top, hair done) to visually signal "after" without overdoing it
- Avoid bright red or colors that clash with FormFocus brand palette (see Mood Board)

**Special equipment recommendations:**
- **Gimbal:** Scene 4 phone POV only — everything else should be handheld or static
- **Ring light or softbox:** Optional — warm window light is preferred, but a 5600K softbox can replicate it for indoor consistency
- **Phone mount or clamp:** For Scene 4 if shooting solo
- **Teleprompter app:** Recommended — lines should feel natural, not read

## Mood Board

**Color palette:**
- `#F5F0EB` — warm off-white/linen (background, clean scenes)
- `#2D6A4F` — deep forest green (FormFocus brand primary — echoed in wardrobe accent or prop)
- `#E9C46A` — warm amber (lighting temperature reference, desk lamp or window glow)

**Lighting mood:** Warm morning productivity — think clean home office, soft golden window light, not harsh or clinical. The feeling of a good morning, not a fluorescent office.

**Creator styling:** Approachable professional. Neat but not stiff. Think "smart friend who has their business together" — not influencer-polished, not completely casual. A linen shirt or structured tee in neutral or earthy tones.

**Set/environment:** Wood-grain desk surface, a few tasteful desk objects (plant, clean notebook, coffee mug). Background slightly soft-focused. The environment should read "intentional home workspace," not "chaotic spare room."

**Visual references:**
- Feels like an **early Notion ad** — clean, warm, real-person energy with a productivity angle
- Lighting and color temperature similar to a **Calm app lifestyle campaign** — soft, unhurried warmth even in a "problem" moment
- Creator performance energy of a **Loom testimonial** — genuine, direct, not performed
