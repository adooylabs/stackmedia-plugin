# Example Output: FormFocus — Instagram Reels Ad Package

**Brand:** FormFocus — AI-powered form builder for small businesses  
**Target Audience:** "Overloaded Olivia" — 28-40 year old small business owner. Juggling client work, admin, and growth. Smart, resourceful, perpetually behind. Uses tools that save time, not tools that require a learning curve.  
**Campaign Goal:** Trials and sign-ups  
**Variations:** 3 (Relatable Pain · Curiosity Gap · Aesthetic Open)

---

## Variation 1 — Relatable Pain Hook

---

**[HOOK - 0:00-0:03]**  
Visual: Creator at a tidy home desk, laptop open, coffee in hand. She looks directly at camera with a tired-but-relatable expression. Soft natural light. Neutral linen background.  
Spoken: "If I have to rebuild that intake form one more time..."  
Caption overlay: **"every small business owner rn"**

**[BODY - 0:03-0:22]**  
Visual: Screen recording overlay on her laptop — showing a clunky form tool with a dozen manual fields. Quick cut to FormFocus dashboard — clean, minimal, AI-suggested fields populating in real time.  
Spoken: "I used to spend an hour building forms every time I onboarded a new client. FormFocus generates the whole thing from a one-line prompt. I just say what the form is for, and it writes the fields, the logic, even the confirmation email."  
Caption overlays:  
- "1 hour → 2 minutes"  
- "AI builds it from a prompt"  
- "fields + logic + follow-up email — done"

**[CTA - 0:22-0:28]**  
Visual: Creator smiles, holds up her phone showing a completed form. Clean product logo end card.  
Spoken: "Link in bio — free trial, no credit card."  
Caption CTA: **"Save this if you're drowning in admin."**  
Link in bio note: formfocus.io/trial

---

**[COVER FRAME CONCEPT]**  
Creator at desk, looking directly to camera with a relatable "done with this" expression. Text overlay in bold sans-serif centered upper-third: **"rebuilding that form for the 4th time"**. Neutral warm tones. Feels like a real person, not an ad.

**[CROSS-POST TO FEED]**  
YES — The pain hook and screen recording demo translate well to Feed. Cover frame works as a static 4:5 thumbnail. The text overlays carry the story for sound-off Feed viewers.

---

**Director's Note:** Cast or direct the creator to look genuinely tired — not performatively frustrated. The comedy is in the understatement. The screen recording should feel like a real workflow comparison, not a polished product demo. Keep the FormFocus UI clean and fast.

---

## Variation 2 — Curiosity Gap Hook

---

**[HOOK - 0:00-0:03]**  
Visual: Close-up on laptop screen — a blinking cursor in a single text field. Nothing else visible. Creator's hands are in frame, she types: "Client onboarding form for a freelance designer." Pull back to reveal FormFocus populating fields in real time.  
Spoken: "Watch what happens when I describe the form instead of building it."  
Caption overlay: **"wait for it..."**

**[BODY - 0:03-0:22]**  
Visual: Continuous screen recording — FormFocus generates 8 fields, conditional logic, a file upload section, and a confirmation email draft. Creator reacts in frame (genuine surprise/delight). Cut to a second example: "Quote request form for a home services business" — different form populates in seconds.  
Spoken: "It figured out every field I would have added manually. The conditional logic, the file upload, the thank-you email — I didn't touch any of it. I've used it for client onboarding, project briefs, event RSVPs. It just... knows."  
Caption overlays:  
- "AI reads your prompt"  
- "generates every field + logic"  
- "works for any type of form"

**[CTA - 0:22-0:28]**  
Visual: Creator leans back from laptop, satisfied. Cut to FormFocus logo and URL.  
Spoken: "Free to try — link in bio."  
Caption CTA: **"Save this for your next form."**  
Link in bio note: formfocus.io/trial

---

**[COVER FRAME CONCEPT]**  
Extreme close-up of laptop screen showing the single text prompt field with the cursor blinking and text half-typed. Text overlay upper-third: **"I described the form. It built itself."** High contrast, slightly mysterious — the curiosity gap starts before the video plays.

**[CROSS-POST TO FEED]**  
YES — Screen recording content reads clearly in Feed. The cover frame text drives clicks from static context. Works well as a retargeting unit for users who've visited the site.

---

**Director's Note:** The key is the reveal pacing — hold on the blank input for a beat longer than feels comfortable, then let the form populate at real speed (don't speed it up). Real-time generation is the wow moment. The creator's genuine reaction on the second example makes it feel unscripted.

## Variation 3 — Aesthetic Open Hook

---

**[HOOK - 0:00-0:03]**  
Visual: No speaking for the first 3 seconds. Wide shot of a beautifully lit home office: warm afternoon light through sheer curtains, a clean desk with a plant, a laptop open to a minimal interface. Slow push toward the screen. The vibe is: this is what a calm, organized business looks like.  
Spoken: (silence, then softly) "My business used to feel like this."  
Caption overlay: **"what it looks like when admin isn't running your life"**

**[BODY - 0:03-0:22]**  
Visual: Creator sits down at the desk, opens FormFocus. She types a prompt — "New client intake form for a brand photographer." Fields appear. She nods slightly. Cut to a montage: phone notification of a completed form, calendar filling with client bookings, creator closing the laptop with an easy smile. Back to her face, speaking to camera.  
Spoken: "I automate my intake forms with FormFocus. One prompt and it builds the whole thing — fields, logic, confirmation email. My clients get something polished. I get my time back. And honestly? My business feels lighter."  
Caption overlays:  
- "one prompt → full form"  
- "professional client experience"  
- "less admin, more business"

**[CTA - 0:22-0:28]**  
Visual: Slow zoom on the clean desk setup. FormFocus URL appears as a tasteful text overlay.  
Spoken: "Free trial — link in bio."  
Caption CTA: **"Save this for when you're ready to simplify."**  
Link in bio note: formfocus.io/trial

---

**[COVER FRAME CONCEPT]**  
Wide, calm home office shot — warm light, clean desk, plant, open laptop with a minimal interface visible. No text overlay on the cover frame (the aesthetic speaks for itself). Feels like a Pinterest board for "organized small business." The visual draws a scroll-stop from aspirational curiosity, not shock.

**[CROSS-POST TO FEED]**  
NO — The aesthetic open relies on the motion of the push-in and the montage sequence to earn the viewer. As a static Feed image, the cover frame is beautiful but lacks a hook. Better suited to Reels discovery than Feed retargeting.

---

**Director's Note:** Lighting is everything in this variation. Shoot in the late afternoon (or replicate warm directional light). The desk must be genuinely clean — not sterile, just considered. The creator's energy is quiet confidence, not excitement. This ad is selling a feeling (calm, in control) as much as a product feature.
