# Example Output: FormFocus — YouTube Shorts Script Package

**Brand:** FormFocus
**Product:** AI form builder
**Target Audience:** "Overloaded Olivia" — a busy operations manager or small business owner drowning in manual intake processes, client onboarding forms, and repetitive data collection. She's tech-comfortable but not a developer. She values time above almost anything else.
**Campaign Goal:** Trial signups
**Variations:** 3 (each uses a different hook type)

---

## Variation 1 — Curiosity Gap Hook

**Hook Mechanic Label:** Curiosity Gap

---

### Script

**[HOOK — 0:00–0:03]**
*Creator on camera, direct eye contact, slightly leaning in. Bold text overlay on screen.*
**On-screen text overlay:** "I deleted my form stack."
**Spoken:** "I deleted every form tool I was using. All of them."

---

**[BODY — 0:03–0:38]**
*Cut to screen recording of FormFocus dashboard — clean, fast, AI field suggestions populating in real time.*
**Spoken:** "I had Typeform for client intake, Google Forms for internal requests, and a Notion template cobbled together for onboarding. Three tools. Three logins. Zero talking to each other."

*Cut back to creator, slightly exasperated expression.*
**Spoken:** "Then I tried FormFocus. It's an AI form builder that actually understands what you're collecting data for — not just what fields to add."

*Back to screen recording: creator typing a prompt — "Client onboarding form for a marketing agency" — and watching a full form generate in seconds.*
**Spoken:** "I typed what I needed. It built the form. Conditional logic included. Pre-filled confirmation emails. Done."

*Cut to creator holding phone, reviewing a form submission notification.*
**Spoken:** "My onboarding time went from 40 minutes per client to about 8. That's not an exaggeration — I timed it."

---

**[SUBSCRIBE CTA — 0:38–0:43]**
*Creator back on camera, relaxed energy.*
**Spoken:** "I'm testing more tools like this every week — subscribe so you actually see the ones worth your time."

---

**[CTA — 0:43–0:50]**
*Bold text overlay animates on screen.*
**On-screen text overlay:** "Try FormFocus free"
**Spoken:** "Link in the description. Free trial, no credit card."
**Description note:** FormFocus free trial — [link]

---

### Thumbnail Frame Concept

**Subject:** Creator leaning toward camera with a mildly dramatic expression — the moment of revelation.
**Text overlay:** "I deleted my form stack." (28 chars)
**Background:** Clean light grey or white wall — minimal, professional.
**Contrast strategy:** Dark charcoal text on light background. Creator wearing a bold solid-color top (deep teal or burgundy) to stand out from the neutral background. No clutter behind them.

---

### Director's Note

**Setting:** Home office or clean desk setup. Minimal but real — a plant or monitor in the background is fine. Avoid green screens.
**Creator energy:** Calm confidence with a hint of "I can't believe I put up with the old way." Not hype. This is an informed professional sharing a discovery.
**Props:** Laptop open to FormFocus dashboard. Phone nearby for the notification shot.
**Long-form clip candidate:** If this campaign includes a full FormFocus walkthrough video, the screen recording segment (0:10–0:30) can be extracted as a standalone "demo Short" with a new hook card added at the start.

## Variation 2 — Relatable Pain Hook

**Hook Mechanic Label:** Relatable Pain

---

### Script

**[HOOK — 0:00–0:03]**
*Creator on camera. Slightly tired expression — not dramatic, just real.*
**On-screen text overlay:** "Rebuilding the same form again?" (36 chars)
**Spoken:** "If you've rebuilt the same intake form three times because someone wanted one more field — this is for you."

---

**[BODY — 0:03–0:35]**
*Cut to screen showing a messy Notion page or a generic Google Form — generic, unbranded.*
**Spoken:** "Most form tools make you build from scratch every time. You're not a developer, but somehow form logic has become your job."

*Cut to FormFocus — creator opens a new form, types a natural-language prompt.*
**Spoken:** "FormFocus flips this. You describe the form you need — in plain English — and the AI builds it. Conditional logic, field validation, confirmation emails — it infers all of it from context."

*Screen recording: editing a single field, adding a conditional branch with one click.*
**Spoken:** "And when someone inevitably asks for one more field? You just tell it what you want. It adjusts. No rebuilding from scratch."

*Back to creator — genuine relief expression.*
**Spoken:** "It's the first form builder that actually feels like it was built for people who have other things to do."

---

**[SUBSCRIBE CTA — 0:35–0:40]**
**Spoken:** "Subscribe — I post tools like this every week for people running lean operations."

---

**[CTA — 0:40–0:48]**
**On-screen text overlay:** "FormFocus — free trial" (22 chars)
**Spoken:** "Try it free — link in the description. Your next form will take four minutes."
**Description note:** FormFocus free trial — [link]

---

### Thumbnail Frame Concept

**Subject:** Creator looking at camera with a relatable "finally" expression — not grinning widely, just a composed nod of relief.
**Text overlay:** "Rebuilding the same form again?" (31 chars)
**Background:** Slightly blurred desk setup with a laptop visible — grounds it in a real workspace.
**Contrast strategy:** White bold text on a slightly darkened background overlay (semi-transparent dark bar behind text). Creator in a light neutral top so they pop against the desk environment.

---

### Director's Note

**Setting:** Desk environment — laptop open, maybe a coffee mug. Real workspace, not staged.
**Creator energy:** Relatable and grounded. This is someone who has been there, not someone selling something. The frustration in the hook should feel earned, not performed.
**Props:** Laptop showing FormFocus. Optional: sticky note on monitor that says "update intake form again" — visual gag that lands without needing to explain it.
**Long-form clip candidate:** The prompt-to-form demo moment (0:15–0:28) works as a standalone 15-second "how it works" Short. Add a text card: "How FormFocus builds forms from a prompt:" and run the demo without narration. Let the screen recording speak.

## Variation 3 — Number-Based Hook

**Hook Mechanic Label:** Number-Based

---

### Script

**[HOOK — 0:00–0:03]**
*Creator on camera. Confident, no-nonsense.*
**On-screen text overlay:** "5 form builders. One winner." (28 chars)
**Spoken:** "I tested five AI form builders so you don't have to. Here's the one that actually won."

---

**[BODY — 0:03–0:40]**
*Quick montage-style cuts: creator briefly showing logos or UI of 4 other tools — 2 seconds each, no audio, just visual + creator reaction (a shrug, a grimace, a "meh" face).*
**Spoken:** "Most of them are just drag-and-drop with an AI label slapped on. They suggest fields. Cool. But you're still the one making decisions."

*Cut to FormFocus demo — creator types a natural-language prompt, full form appears.*
**Spoken:** "FormFocus is different because it understands intent. I typed 'client onboarding form for a design studio' and it built conditional logic based on project type — without me asking."

*Screen recording: showing the completed form, the email confirmation sequence, the embed code.*
**Spoken:** "It also connects to your existing tools — Slack notification on submission, data straight to your spreadsheet. Setup took me eleven minutes total."

*Back to creator.*
**Spoken:** "Out of five tools, this is the only one I'm still using a month later. That's the test that matters."

---

**[SUBSCRIBE CTA — 0:40–0:45]**
**Spoken:** "Subscribe — I do these comparisons every month. The good tools deserve to be found."

---

**[CTA — 0:45–0:52]**
**On-screen text overlay:** "Try FormFocus free — link below" (31 chars)
**Spoken:** "Free trial in the description. Try it on one real form and see what happens."
**Description note:** FormFocus free trial — [link] | Full comparison writeup: [link]

---

### Thumbnail Frame Concept

**Subject:** Creator looking directly at camera with a slight knowing smile — the "I've done the work for you" energy.
**Text overlay:** "5 form builders. One winner." (28 chars)
**Background:** Clean, slightly warm-toned wall. Minimal.
**Contrast strategy:** Bold white text with a thin dark stroke/shadow so it reads over any background tone. Creator in a high-contrast outfit — dark navy or black top against a lighter wall. Number "5" could be visually larger than surrounding text if the editor can customize the overlay treatment.

---

### Director's Note

**Setting:** Clean desk or standing setup. Slightly more polished than the relatable-pain variation — this is a reviewer, not just a user sharing a find.
**Creator energy:** Authoritative but approachable. "I did the research, here's the answer." No overselling — the confidence comes from having done the comparison, not from being paid to say it.
**Props:** Laptop. Optional: printed or on-screen "scorecard" graphic comparing the 5 tools — adds visual texture during the comparison section.
**Long-form clip candidate:** This script was designed to be extracted from a long-form "Best AI Form Builders 2025" comparison video. The FormFocus-specific segment (0:10–0:40) maps directly to a chapter in the long-form review. Timestamp the long-form chapter at the same demo beat so the Short can link back to the full video with "Watch the full comparison — link below."
