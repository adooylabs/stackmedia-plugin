## Instagram Post (1080x1080) — Variation 1

**Dimensions:** 1080x1080 px
**Format:** JPG
**File name:** formfocus-instagram-post-v1.jpg

### Layout

**Background:** Light background `#F8FAFC`, full bleed.

**Foreground (center):** Person at laptop, cropped from mid-chest up, positioned slightly left-of-center. Expression is relieved and happy — looking at the laptop screen. The laptop screen shows a clean, well-structured form interface (product UI).

**Right third:** Text block stacked vertically in the right third of the frame. Vertical blue accent line (`#2563EB`, 4px wide) runs the full height of the text block on the left edge of the text zone, creating a visual divider between image and copy.

**Bottom strip (last 120px):** Solid `#2563EB` bar spanning the full width. Contains the CTA text (white) on the left and the FormFocus logo (white) on the right.

### Text Layers

| Layer | Copy | Font Style | Size | Color | Position |
|-------|------|-----------|------|-------|----------|
| Headline | Stop dreading your forms. | Bold sans-serif | 52px | `#0F172A` | Right third, upper half, left-aligned |
| Subhead | FormFocus builds smart forms that actually convert — in minutes. | Regular sans-serif | 24px | `#0F172A` at 70% opacity | Right third, below headline, left-aligned |
| Accent tag | AI-powered | Bold sans-serif, uppercase, small | 18px | `#10B981` | Above headline, left-aligned |
| CTA | Try it free → | Bold sans-serif | 26px | `#F8FAFC` | Bottom blue strip, left side |
| Logo | N/A | N/A | 32px height | White | Bottom blue strip, right side, right-aligned |

### Visual Direction

Warm, natural lifestyle photography. The person is the emotional anchor — their relieved, happy expression is the hook. Laptop screen should show FormFocus UI (a clean multi-step form with blue accents). Lighting: bright, soft studio or natural window light from the left. No dark or dramatic tones — this is a "this is easy and good" feeling.

Color palette application:
- Background: `#F8FAFC` (airy, approachable)
- Accent line: `#2563EB` (brand blue — signals product)
- Bottom bar: `#2563EB` (grounds the composition, contains brand)
- Accent tag: `#10B981` (green = positive signal, "AI-powered" is a trust signal)

### Brand Application

- Headlines use the boldest weight of the brand sans-serif (e.g., Inter Bold or DM Sans Bold)
- Body text uses regular weight, slightly reduced opacity to create visual hierarchy without a second color
- Brand blue appears in 2 places: accent line and bottom bar — consistent without being overdone
- Accent green used once, on the "AI-powered" tag only — keeps it feeling premium, not cluttered

### Canva Template Recommendation

Search Canva for: **"Minimalist product feature Instagram post with photo left and text right"** or **"Clean tech startup Instagram square with bottom color bar"**. Look for templates with a strong left/right split layout, not a centered hero layout.

### Designer Notes

- Keep the person's face in the left half of the image — do not let it overlap the text zone on the right
- The blue accent line is critical — it signals where the "product zone" ends and the "brand message zone" begins
- Export as JPG at high quality. Target file size under 2 MB for fast feed loading
- Test the design at 400x400px preview size — the person's expression and the headline must both read clearly

## YouTube Thumbnail — Variation 1

**Dimensions:** 1280x720 px
**Format:** JPG
**File name:** formfocus-youtube-thumbnail-v1.jpg

### Layout

**Left 55% (photo zone):** Person at laptop, happy/surprised expression — high energy face clearly visible, cropped from shoulders up. Bright, slightly over-exposed photo treatment to maximize thumbnail pop. Yellow-to-orange gradient halo behind the person's head (subtle, adds depth and visual warmth against the blue right side).

**Right 45% (text zone):** Solid `#2563EB` background. Large, bold, white text, stacked on 2 lines. FormFocus logo in the bottom-right corner of the text zone, small.

**Dividing edge:** A slight diagonal lean to the right (~5 degrees) on the border between photo and text zone — feels dynamic, not static.

### Text Layers

| Layer | Copy | Font Style | Size | Color | Position |
|-------|------|-----------|------|-------|----------|
| Line 1 | BETTER | Extra bold / black weight, all caps | 112px | `#F8FAFC` | Right zone, top third, left-aligned |
| Line 2 | FORMS | Extra bold / black weight, all caps | 112px | `#10B981` | Right zone, below line 1, left-aligned |
| Line 3 subtitle | in 5 minutes | Bold sans-serif, mixed case | 48px | `#F8FAFC` at 85% opacity | Right zone, below line 2, left-aligned |
| Logo | N/A | N/A | 24px height | White at 70% opacity | Bottom right corner of text zone |

### Visual Direction

High contrast, high energy. The person's expression is the emotional hook — they should look genuinely delighted (not posed), as if they just saw a result they didn't expect. Photo treatment: boost contrast, slightly increase saturation, brighten mid-tones. No filters that make it look "produced" — it should feel authentic.

The right side blue zone is a flat `#2563EB` with no texture — purity and confidence. The `#10B981` green on "FORMS" creates a color pop against the blue that is highly visible even at small thumbnail size.

### Brand Application

- Extra bold/black weight for thumbnail text — regular or medium weight will not read at 320x180
- Two-color text treatment: white for "BETTER", green for "FORMS" — keeps it visually interesting and makes "FORMS" (the product category) pop
- Logo is present but quiet — this is not a branding asset, it's a conversion asset

### Canva Template Recommendation

Search Canva for: **"Bold YouTube thumbnail with face on left and text block on right, solid color background"** or **"High contrast split YouTube thumbnail with expression photo and large text."** Look for templates that dedicate the left 50-60% to an image and the right to oversized text.

### Designer Notes

- **Test at 320x180 before finalizing:** shrink the design to 25% in Canva's preview. "BETTER FORMS" must read clearly. "in 5 minutes" is secondary — acceptable if it's hard to read at tiny size.
- Thumbnail text total word count: 3 words — within the 6-word limit
- If the person photo has a busy or neutral background, use Canva's Background Remover tool to isolate the subject and place them against the gradient halo element directly
- Export as JPG. Check file size — YouTube hard limit is 2 MB. If over, reduce quality to 80% in Canva's download dialog.
- Contrast check: white on `#2563EB` = approximately 5.9:1 (passes WCAG AA). Green (`#10B981`) on `#2563EB` = approximately 2.8:1 — acceptable for very large display text (112px), but do not use this combination for small text.
