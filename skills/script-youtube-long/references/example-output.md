## Director's Note

**Setting:** Home office or kitchen table — Sunday morning feel. Natural light preferred. Warm and lived-in, not styled. The authenticity of the environment supports the story.

**Wardrobe:** Casual — oversized sweater or relaxed shirt. Avoid branded clothing. The vibe is "real person sharing a discovery," not "influencer unboxing."

**Key props:** Laptop (FormFocus open), coffee mug (half-drunk, realistic), optional: small stack of papers or notebook nearby to reinforce the "admin overload" story.

**Screen recording setup:** Record FormFocus demo separately at high resolution (1080p minimum). Use a clean browser window — no distracting tabs. Have the AI generation demo queued and ready to run live during recording for authentic reaction capture.

**Shoot order recommendation:**
1. Record screen demo footage first (FormFocus AI generation, interface walkthrough) — this is the hardest to reshoot.
2. Record talking head segments with demo footage fresh in mind so reactions feel genuine.
3. Capture B-roll last: messy Google Drive folder, notification sounds, "before" footage.

**Shorts flagged:** Timestamps 0:00–0:35, 1:45–2:30, and 4:45–5:10 are all extraction-ready. Minimal re-editing required — add text overlays and vertical crop.

**Mid-roll note:** If this video runs 8+ minutes in final form, plan a secondary persuasion beat (a quick "still watching? Here's the one thing I haven't mentioned yet...") after the 8-minute mark to recapture viewers returning from a mid-roll ad break.
